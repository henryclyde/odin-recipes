Air Fryer Chicken Fajitas

Air fried chicken tastes great, it's hard to mess up, and prep time and cleanup time are very quick.


Ingredients

  • Raw chicken breast, flattened
  • Chopped onion
  • Chopped bell pepper
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Taco seasoning
  • Olive oil
  • Ranch (or dipping sauce of choice)

Steps

  1. Season chicken breast with salt, pepper, and taco seasoning
  2. Inside air fryer basket, pour olive oil over chicken breast
  3. Put in sliced onion and peppers
  4. Mix around to coat everything in olive oil
  5. Heat for 15 minutes at 400, flipping chicken breast halfway through
